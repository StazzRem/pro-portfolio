About Me

My name is Stacey Remnant

I grew up in Cornwall, UK, which is a beautiful part of the world. After many years of ups and downs, I moved to the Netherlands for love in 2021.

While starting to settle within a new country, I had time to explore my skills and strengths - prompting me to return to my college roots and learn how to code. I started off with learning Java, and soon reignited my passion for technical building. In July 2022, I enrolled in a Pro course with SheCodes and learnt Front End Development, delving into HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

I'm a passionate creative, and turning code into something visually pleasing is really exciting! I have aspirations to learn other programming languages to create not only websites, but apps and even games.

Fun facts:

  • I enjoy cosplay and body painting
  • I fundraised over £4,000 in one year for charities
  • I used to be a broadcaster on Twitch, streaming games and art
  • I studied Digital Animation at University, but never graduated
